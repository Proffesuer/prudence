MISS PRUDENCE TENDWA

Prudence Tendwa is a passionate model who began her modeling career at age of 18.
She made History when she became the first to be crowned
Miss Catholic University Of Eastern
Eldoret Campus.

Early Life

Tendwa was born on 24th Sep 1999 in Kakamega county Kenya.
She grey up in Nairobi City and later her family moved permanently to Trans-Nzoia county after
her secondary education.
In 2018 she joined The Catholic University Of Estern Africa Kenya to parsue a
Bachelor Degree specializing in Accounting.

TOP SUPERMODE

Prudence Tendwa is one of the renowned models in trans-Nzoia and Uasin Gishu counties,
after bagging several tittles in these regions.
She is The founder of STOP THE RED SPOT CAMPAIGN IN UASIN GISHU COUNTY
and is on the run to bring the same initiative to her home county Trans-Nzoia Kenya.
